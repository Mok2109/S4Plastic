WELCOME!

Plastic Project is one of the main things we should use to make the world a better place. The website has many different features that make it user and earth-friendly. You can check whether your plastic is recyclable and which place you can drop that plastic at because some places only recycle certain numbers of plastics. The rest gets sent out to the dump yard. This feature can reduce the amount of plastic going to the dump yard. Our website also shows places where you can donate items that you can reuse, reduce, and recycle. This feature helps reduce the amount of plastic debris in oceans and other areas. We also have a shop where you can sell plastic items that are in good shape for less than the original price. This feature helps you get some money and others get inexpensive items. We also give out a place where you can watch videos on how plastic affects the Earth. Our app will definitely make a difference in our environment.
